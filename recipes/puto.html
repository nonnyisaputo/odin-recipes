Puto

Description

Filipino steamed, slightly sweet rice cake made from ground rice. Can be eaten with savory or sweet flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg white
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 110g (1 cup) rice flour
  • 6.5g (2 tsp) baking powder
  • 112g white sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat steamer over medium heat on the stove.
  2. Whisk egg whites, water, and coconut milk until fully incorporated.
  3. Sift rice flour and baking powder over the wet ingredients. Whisk to combine.
  4. Add sugar in the batter. Mix to combine.
  5. Scoopt into mini muffin molds, 3/4 of the way up. Place molds into steamer and cover with lid.
  6. Steam for 8 minutes and remove lid to see if puto is fluffy and firm.
  7. Remove from steamer and allow puto to rest in molds for 30 seconds to cool. Remove from molds. Serve immediately.
