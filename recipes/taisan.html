Taisan

Description

Soft and fluffy chiffon cake. Brushed with butter then sprinkled with sugar and cheese on top.

Ingredients

Cake Batter

  • 1 1/4 cup cake flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 6 egg yolks (room temperature)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup milk (room temperature)

Meringue

  • 6 egg whites (room temperature)
  • 1.4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cream of tartar

Toppings

  • unsalted butter
  • granulated sugar
  • grated cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325F. Line 9x5 loaf pan with parchment paper with a slight overhang.
  2. Whisk egg whites with cream of tartar in stand mixer on low-medium until foamy.
  3. Slowly add sugar one teaspoon at a time. Whisk until stiff peaks form.
  4. In another bowl, whisk egg yolks until smooth and thick.
  5. Add sugar and continue to whisk until blended.
  6. Add water, oil, vanilla extract, melted butter and milk. Whisk until combined.
  7. Sift flour, baking powder, and salt directly onto egg yolk mixture. Stir until incorporated.
  8. Gently fold meringue into batter in thirds. Fold until there are no streaks of egg whites.
  9. Transfer batter into prepared pan and bake 30-40 minutes or until cake springs back when gently pressed. A toothpick inserted into the center should come out dry with minimal dry crumbs.
  10. Remove pan from oven and allow to cool for a few minutes. While the cake is still warm, brush with butter and sprinkle with sugar and cheese.
  11. Enjoy!
